What Mentorship Means Here

Mentorship at AdeptFlux isn't about me reading a textbook to you. It is a transfer of experience.

It is about taking the chaotic mix of theories you learned in university and organizing them into a structured "Engineering Mindset" that you can use to solve problems you've never seen before. It is the bridge between "I think the answer is 5V" and "Based on the input swing and headroom, I designed the stage for 5V."

How Sessions Are Structured

We strip away the fluff. Every session is designed to simulate a real-world design review or a high-stakes interview environment.
